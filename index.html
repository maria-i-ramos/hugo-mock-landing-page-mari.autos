Penn Course Review Extension

Get Started

Open-source MIT Licensed.

Course Selection Initiator

Start your comparison by selecting a course on Path@Penn The extension prompts you to choose your first course, enhancing the original functionality.

Integrated Highlight Detection

Highlight a course directly on Path@Penn — the extension automatically scrapes Penn Course Review for the highlighted course.

AI-Powered Recommendation Prompt

Combining your preferences with Penn Course Review data, the extension generates a preformatted LLM prompt to help you determine which course fits better.

Persistent Course Storage 🦉

Unlike the original fork, our version stores data from multiple courses, allowing for a reliable two-course comparison instead of just one at a time.

Second Course Comparison Prompt ✨

Get prompted to select your second course to compare. This step expands beyond the original functionality by supporting a dual-course analysis.

Preference-Based Matching 🧰

The extension asks: “What are you looking for in a class?” It gathers your interests (e.g., rigor, instructor quality) to shape personalized insights.
